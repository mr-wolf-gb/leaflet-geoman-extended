Click the Distance Line button (line icon with ruler) in the toolbar to start!

How it works:

  • Click the Distance Line button
  • Enter a distance interval (e.g., 2)
  • Select unit (km or m)
  • Click Start Drawing
  • Click on the map to place vertices
  • Labels show cumulative distances (2km, 4km, 6km...)
  • Double-click to finish drawing
